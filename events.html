League of Legends Worlds 2021 was the most watched esports event of 2021 - it had more than 174.8 million Hours Watched. It is not only the highest total for this year, but the highest of all time! The previous record belonged to Worlds 2020, and the new Worlds accumulated almost 25% more Hours Watched.
